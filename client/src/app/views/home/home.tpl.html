About

MedCheck is an openFDA driven solution that provides you with a safety assessment of your drug of interest. Pulling data from the adverse event and recall data from openFDA’s drug dataset, MedCheck provides a holistic and filterable view of a drug’s adverse events and recalls.

Learn

An Adverse Event Report is submitted to the FDA to report any undesirable experience associated with the use of a medical product in a patient. A report may list several drug products, as well as several patient reactions. Recalls are actions taken by a firm to remove a product from the market. Recalls may be conducted on a firm's own initiative, by FDA request, or by FDA order under statutory authority.

Explore

MedCheck pulls and displays adverse event and recall data from openFDA. MedCheck allows you to filter the data to narrow down a drug’s data to a desired subset. For example, a mother of a sick child may want to review adverse event and recall data involving patients ages 6 and below.
